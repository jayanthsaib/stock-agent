What Cerebro Does

Everything you need to trade smarter

From raw market data to an approved trade — fully automated, fully auditable

AI Signal Generation

Fundamental analysis, technical indicators (RSI, EMA, MACD), and macro context all feed into a single confidence score for each stock.

15+ Hard Risk Rules

Position sizing, portfolio drawdown limits, sector concentration caps, R:R ratio gates — every signal must pass all rules before reaching you.

Telegram Approval

Receive a full signal report on Telegram — entry, target, stop-loss, confidence breakdown. Reply APPROVE or REJECT. You're always in control.

Live Portfolio Monitor

Open positions tracked every 15 minutes with live prices and P&L. Automatic stop-loss and target-hit detection with instant Telegram alerts.

Backtesting Engine

Run the full strategy against 1-year historical OHLCV data. Get win rate, average return, max drawdown, and Sharpe ratio before risking real capital.

Self-Learning Engine

Monthly reviews analyse which signals succeeded and failed. Signal weights are automatically adjusted to improve accuracy over time.

The Process

How Cerebro Works

Four automated steps from raw data to an executed trade

1

Scan Market

Phase 1+2 ingestion scans 500+ NSE stocks — batch price quotes, volume filters, 1-year OHLCV history loaded via Angel One SmartAPI.

2

AI Analysis

Fundamental, technical, and macro modules run in parallel. Each stock receives a composite confidence score. Only top candidates proceed.

3

Risk Validation

15+ hard rules filter every signal. Entry price, target, stop-loss, and capital allocation are calculated automatically for each trade.

4

You Decide

A full signal report lands in your Telegram. APPROVE to place the order via Angel One SmartAPI. REJECT to skip. Your capital, your call.
