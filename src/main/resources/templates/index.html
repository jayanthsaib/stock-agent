How Cerebro Works
Four steps from raw data to executed trade
1

Scan

Phase 1+2 data ingestion scans 500+ NSE stocks — price filters, volume filters, 1-year OHLCV history.

2

Analyse

Fundamental, technical, and macro modules score each stock in parallel. Only high-conviction candidates proceed.

3

Validate

15+ hard risk rules filter out bad setups. Entry, target, stop-loss, and position size are calculated automatically.

4

Execute

You approve or reject on Telegram. Approved signals are placed as paper or live orders via Angel One SmartAPI.
